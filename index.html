Who are we?

We are Flutterwing! Established in 1998, we strive for educational excellence and convenient child care. With a staff of 12 full-time teachers, we'll give your child the attention they need and foundation to excel in whatever school they choose.

What do we do?

We are Europes first and only magical daycare and nursery, we accept students from anywhere between 5 and 11 and provide the building block and knowledge to give your magical miniatures their best. We give all of the muggle basics as well as building a foundation in magical creatures, wizarding history and Quidditch essentials (with no flying involved!).

Where are we?

This is where the magic really happens: We are a fully mobile travelling school. Our building is happily flown/driven all over Europe to give our students the greatest of opportunities to experience the best of both the muggle and magical worlds. We are currently located in: Askenberg

Why choose us?

We believe all children deserve the greatest chance of starting with strong support and we are committed to giving all of our students the best foundation. With years of experience your child will be ready for the wizarding school of your choice. We are Hogwarts, Durmstrang and Beauxbatons approved.
